Frieren

She is Frieren, protagonist of tales beyond journey's end. Mage of the Hero's band, she walked with Himmel, the valiant; Eisen, the stalwart; and Heiter, blessed of the cloth, a decade past to vanquish the Demon King.

Upon Himmel's fading, Frieren doth tread forth with Fern and Stark, seeking Aureole, to commune once more with echoes of Himmel's departed soul.

Species: Elf

Class: Mage

Gender: Female

Age: 1000+ years; Alive.

Fern.

Fern, a fledgling sorceress of human stock, doth attend Frieren as her apprentice and comrade. An orphan, forged in the fires of war from the Southern Lands, she was taken in by Heiter, and upon his passing, entrusted to Frieren's watch.

Species: Human

Class: Mage

Gender: Female

Age: 19-20 years; Alive

Stark

Hark, Stark, a warrior of man. When demons assailed his village, he did flee, 'ere to be Eisen's charge. By Eisen's command, he did join Frieren's company, as their vanguard to serve.

Species Human

Class: Warrior, Vanguard

Gender: Male

Age: 20 years; Alive

Himmel.

Himmel, he was the Hero of the fellowship esteemed. With Heiter the priest; Frieren the mage; and Eisen the warrior, the Demon King was vanquished, and peace wrought upon the land. Now, in death's embrace he lies, claimed by age. Frieren, stirred by this fate, doth seek to know Mankind, thus setting forth the tale of the journey's end.

Species: Human

Class: Warrior, Hero

Gender: Male

Age: 76 years; Deceased

Eisen.

Eisen, a dwarf he is, once warrior of the Hero's band, who vanquished the Demon King, with Frieren, Himmel, and Heiter at his side. Though dwarves live long by human measure, 'tis shorter still than the Elves' eternal march.

Species: Dwarf

Class: Warrior

Gender: Male

Age: 100+ years; Alive

Heiter.

Heiter, a priestly man of the Hero's band. When the fellowship disbanded, he did return to Strahl, and did rise to bishop. In his twilight, he took Fern, a babe orphaned, as his own. When Frieren visit, twenty years after Himmel's passing, he bid her adopt Fern, and guide her staff.

Species: Human

Class: Priest

Gender: Male

Age: 99-100 years; Deceased
